We offer industrial grade coats such as 3M™ Scotchkote™ 134.

  • Resistant to corrosive soils, hydrocarbons, harsh chemicals and sea water
  • Meets the requirements of AWWA Standard C213 and C550
  • Long-term performance history in water, sewage, and other service environments

    • 3M™ Scotchkote™ Fusion-Bonded Epoxy Coating 134 is a one part, heat curable epoxy coating that can be custom applied to protect pipelines (external and internal) and a variety of infrastructure components from corrosion.


    The epoxy is applied to preheated steel as a dry powder which melts and cures to a uniform coating thickness. This bonding process provides excellent adhesion and coverage on applications such as valves, pumps, pipe drains, hydrants and porous castings. Scotchkote Coating 134 is resistant to wastewater, corrosive soils, hydrocarbons, harsh chemicals, and sea water. Powder properties allow easy manual or automatic application by electrostatic or air-spray equipment.
