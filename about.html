Mission: Market leader in West Texas region for high quality commercial and retail coatings while continually improving the business in all aspects. Sustain a company that Customers recommend, Companies prefer, Suppliers select, and Employees are proud.

We are Texas Tech alumni owned and operated. Our business centers around family, for this is the base of how we conduct business and treat each other at work. Coating Specialists brings over 5 years of powder coating, manufacturing and client focus to all projects. We work with any customer with a projects from the pump industry, oil and gas, general contracting, home builders, fabricators, and manufactures. We are committed at every level of our processes to exceed customer expectations. We are a full service coating facility which offers Abrasive Blasting, Powder Coating, Cerakoting, Hydro-Graphics, and Industrial Coating. As a member of the WTHBA, BBB, and PCI(Powder Coating Institute), Coating Specialists tailors ideal coating solutions to meet each project’s specific need, while maintaining the highest level of quality.
